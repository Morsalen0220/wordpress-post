1️⃣ What a PDF Editor Can Edit

An experienced pdf editor can modify non-editable files while preserving style, spacing, and design consistency.

  • Text correction, typo fixes, and grammar cleanup in scanned PDFs.
  • Name, date, amount, and address updates for business documents.
  • Font detection and realistic font matching.
  • Paragraph alignment, spacing, and margin balancing.
  • Background noise cleanup and scan quality enhancement.
  • Table cleanup, header/footer edits, and section reflow.
  • Logo replacement and brand color correction.
  • Low-quality layout reconstruction from image sources.

Supported files: scanned PDF, locked PDF screenshots, PSD, JPG, PNG, TIFF, and camera-captured pages.
